The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it was selectively bred from an extinct population of wolves during the Late Pleistocene by hunter-gatherers.

The cat, also referred to as domestic cat or house cat, is a small domesticated carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species of the family Felidae.

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae, which is in the order Lagomorpha. They are familiar throughout the world as a wild prey animal, a domesticated form of livestock, and a pet
